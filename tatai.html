Tatai

Tatai is Maori Speech Recognition Software Mathematical pronunciation Aid, which is used by children to allow them to practice their maths as well as their Te Reo at the same time.
Tatai runs on all Linux/Unix based operating systems with the support of JavaFX and HTK Software.

The features of this application include:
  • Recognition of Te Reo numbers between 1-99
  • Leaderboard and stats unique to each user
  • Multiple User Functionality

  • Custom equation creator
  • Notifications on personal bests & high scores
